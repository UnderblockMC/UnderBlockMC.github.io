Core Gameplay

Everything revolves around risk, money, and territory.

💊 Drug Empire

Run product, control labs, and sell through dealers in districts where prices and risk change constantly.

🔫 Custom Guns

Use custom weapons with your combat system for close-range fights, bounty hunts, and gang wars.

🏙 District Control

Fight for sections of the city and take ownership for bonuses, income, and better local market control.

👮 Heat & Police

Crime raises your wanted level. If police see you, the pressure escalates. Hide long enough and lose them.

🎯 Bounties

Players can place money on enemies. Take them out, claim their head, and redeem the reward.

🏦 ATMs & Robberies

Use ATMs to move cash, but high-risk areas can turn every transaction into an ambush.
